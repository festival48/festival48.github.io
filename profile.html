About

The 48 Dance Festival was founded in 1997. This year we will celebrate our 20th anniversary with a diverse programme reflecting the artistic development of the festival and showcasing the various trends in contemporary dance over the last two decades. Full line-up to be announced soon!

Previous performers include

  • ABC Dance Company
  • We Can't Dance
  • Abstract Dance Collective
